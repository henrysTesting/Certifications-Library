# Generative AI Concepts

## Core Topics

- **Prompt engineering** — zero-shot, few-shot, chain-of-thought, system prompts, temperature, top-p
- **7 prompting principles** — clarity/specificity, context, role prompting, few-shot, chain-of-thought, iterative refinement, negative prompting
- **Fine-tuning** — full fine-tune vs. PEFT, LoRA, QLoRA, knowledge distillation
- **RLHF** — reinforcement learning from human feedback, reward models, PPO, alignment; annotator bias risk
- **Hallucination** — causes, detection, mitigation (RAG, lower temp, chain-of-thought)
- **Diffusion models** — forward noise → reverse denoise; image/audio generation
- **Foundation models** — single vs. multimodal; versatile, trained on diverse data
