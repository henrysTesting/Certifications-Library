# NCA-GENL — NVIDIA Certified Associate: Generative AI LLMs

## Overview

The NCA-GENL validates foundational knowledge of large language models and generative AI, with an emphasis on NVIDIA's ecosystem and tooling.

## Exam Domains

| Domain | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| [[LLM Fundamentals]] | Transformer architecture, tokenization, embeddings, training |
| [[Generative AI Concepts]] | Prompt engineering, inference, fine-tuning, RLHF |
| [[NVIDIA AI Stack]] | NIM, NeMo, TensorRT-LLM, CUDA for AI |
| [[Deployment and Optimization]] | Quantization, batching, latency/throughput tradeoffs |
| [[RAG and Applications]] | Retrieval-augmented generation, vector databases, pipelines |
